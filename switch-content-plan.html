Compliance — applies to every piece

  • $221/yr loyalty penalty = ACCC 2025. Always attribute — never present as Homerun research
  • 69% haven't compared = 2026 survey of 3,672 AU sharehousers. Cite source on external use
  • No "guaranteed savings", "cheapest", "best deal", "whole of market" — ever
  • No "cash back", "cashback", "savings toward deposit" — hard ban
  • All savings framing: "see if you're overpaying" not "you will save $X"
  • General energy-switching copy = no CIMET sign-off needed. Only copy naming CIMET requires it
  • Rewards wording: "rewards" / "value back" / "perks" — never "cashback" or "savings toward deposit"
  • Time claim: use "under 3 minutes" across organic — matches the messaging matrix. Not "60 seconds" (paid ad S17 uses this for entry step only)
