Standing rules

Apply to all Switch content

Never name CIMET — refer to "partner retailers" or "Homerun Switch" only.
$221 — ACCC loyalty-tax stat. Question/conditional framing only: "Could your house be paying $221 more?" Never "save $221".
Never claim whole-of-market — Switch compares a panel of partner retailers, not every plan.
Caption disclosure: "We compare a panel of partner retailers and may earn a commission if you switch."
Never: "guaranteed savings", "cheapest", "best deal", "cash back".
All Switch content requires Scott approval before posting.
